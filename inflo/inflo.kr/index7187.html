I had a forehead lift and lipofilling with INFLO

Author:Daria D / Date:2024-08-06 05:54:45 / Views:1,193

I have already done blepharoplasty but as you can see the problem has returned and I remembered how in my facial structure the surgeon recommended that I have a forehead and eyebrow lift as my face is prone to drooping eyelids. The surgeon promised that he would correct the asymmetry of my eyebrows and the result would be fixed. He said that the final result would be in 6 months. At first it may seem to you that after the surgery your forehead is big. Don't be afraid - it's just swelling. The forehead will return to its previous state but your eyelids will be tightened. You will rarely find this information on the Internet and it is very important to hear from someone after the surgery. I was also nervous, but my forehead became the same, it didn't become big but I got an open look although my face always looked tired even at 15 years old. I recommend doing this to people who have a hereditary drooping eyelid. I recommend doing a forehead and eyebrow lift before you do a pointless blepharoplasty.. The surgeon always has the patience to repeat to you and calm you down even if you are not calm. He is a good doctor and I trust him.
