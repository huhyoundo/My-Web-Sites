I had a revision rhinoplasty consultation at AB Plastic Surgery.

Author:dltjdus / Date:2025-05-28 15:56:46 / Views:130

My first surgery was done in my home country, but I wasn't happy with the results, so I looked into options in Korea. I heard AB specializes in revision cases, so I decided to visit them.

It was nice that a translator was there to help during the consultation, and the doctor really seemed to understand my situation. He mentioned that I have a lot of scar tissue, so the surgery would need to be approached carefully.

To be honest, I'm still a bit nervous, but the way everything was explained made me feel reassured. I'm still considering the surgery.
